Celeste

Total Hours Played:

52.1

Rating:

9.7

Celeste is definitely my favorite 2d platforming title. Every aspect of this game is great. First of all, the story is really beautiful. It goes over a woman, Madeline, overcoming personal struggles to climb a mountain. On her journey, she meets some friends and foes. Another amazing part of this game is the music. Lena Raine, the producer for this game, made some absolutely fantastic songs. First steps, for example, is an amazing playlist-worthy song. Finally, my favorite thing that Celeste has to offer is its difficult platforming. I have completed all of the game's challenges except for the golden strawberries (deathless runs.) Playing the free Farewell DLC was absolutely great. There were some very difficult, yet rewarding sections. I am so glad that I was able to conquer the challenges of this game.
