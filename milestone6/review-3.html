Half-Life 2

Total Hours Played:

16.8

Rating:

10

Half Life 2 is my favorite videogame ever. With it being the second game in the series, the developers somehow went from an amazing first game to a breathtaking masterpiece. Half Life 2 was really ahead of its time. When I play the game, the graphics still hold up and rival those of modern titles. With the game being released in 2004, you would not really expect it to be as amazing as it is. Another amazing part of this game is its very creative story. Basically, a government-funded science facility, Black Mesa, tested some alien crystals. When they tested these crystals, it created a portal for aliens to come through. The second game takes place many years after that incident. Aliens have already taken over everything. It is Gordon Freeman's job to somehow figure out a way to prevent the aliens from terrorizing the humans any further.
